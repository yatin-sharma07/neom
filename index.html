Port of NEOM

Open for business

Operating 24/7 with expanded operations, Port of NEOM is facilitating trade across northwest Saudi Arabia and strengthening connectivity through intermodal corridors linking Egypt, the region, and Europe. 

As the Port expands with an advanced container terminal and deeper integration with Oxagon’s industrial ecosystem, emerging as a gateway for Saudi Arabia’s maritime and trade ambitions.

A REGION IN THE MAKING

NEOM is enabled by an ecosystem of sectors, companies and investments

  • Mobility

    NEOM is connecting people and services, aiming to create a smart, shared and sustainable public transport system – powered by renewable energy.

  • Water

    NEOM is building a sustainable water management system that aims to eliminate waste and prevent pollution for future communities.

  • Energy

    NEOM is investing in green energy solutions which will harness our rich, renewable natural resources and power the cities of the future.

  • Tech and Digital

    NEOM is developing the digital infrastructure to enable our emerging regions and the smart cities of the future.
